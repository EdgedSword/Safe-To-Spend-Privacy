Privacy Policy

SafeToSpend is designed with privacy in mind. Your financial data belongs to you.

Data Collection

SafeToSpend does not collect, track, or sell personal data. All budgeting and expense data you enter is stored locally on your device.

iCloud Sync

If iCloud is enabled on your device, SafeToSpend uses Apple’s iCloud (CloudKit) to securely sync your data across your own devices. This data is associated with your Apple ID and is not accessible to the developer.

In-App Purchases

SafeToSpend offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock Pro features. Purchases are processed securely by Apple via the App Store. The developer does not receive or store payment information.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are scheduled locally on your device using Apple’s notification services. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Widgets

Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets access the same local or iCloud-synced data used by the app. No additional data collection occurs.

Third-Party Services

SafeToSpend does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking frameworks, or data brokers.

Data Sharing

Your data is never shared with third parties. Exported data (such as CSV files) is generated only at your request and remains under your control.
